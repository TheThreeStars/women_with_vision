Featured Projects

Teen Wellness Program (Since 2015)

Held annually in Mthatha, this program provides teenagers with health check-ups by local doctors, educational sessions on hygiene, and dignity packs containing essential hygiene items.

Cervical Cancer Awareness Campaigns

Launched in Mthatha in 2021, this initiative educates women aged 21 and above about the importance of pap smear tests and early detection, addressing a critical gap in cervical cancer awareness. Thank you to Dr. Giyose and her team for dedicating their time.
