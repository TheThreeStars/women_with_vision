Together we can make a difference!

Women With Vision is dedicated to uplifting underserved communities by building partnerships, nurturing children, and inspiring meaningful change.

Make a Difference

At Women With Vision, we believe in the transformative power of compassion and action

Across South Africa, our programs in education, nourishment, wellness, and joy uplift underserved communities, creating opportunities for them to thrive.

Join us as we work to break barriers, inspire hope, and build brighter futures. Together, we can make a difference that lasts a lifetime.

What We Do:

  • Empower children through Education
  • Feed minds and bodies with Nourishment
  • Promote holistic health through Wellness
  • Spread happiness and hope with Joy

Our Active Missions

Mthatha Papsmear Campaign

To date, we have screened almost 600 ladies, and we remain committed to running educational campaigns to raise awareness about the importance of pap smears for adult women.

Children’s Christmas Parties

We host Christmas parties for children in underserved communities each year.

Mandela Day Celebrations

Every year on Mandela Day, July 18th, our WWV branches across the country dedicate their 67 minutes by cooking meals, gifting art supplies, and spending time with the elderly in old age homes.
